1) BUY BEARCOIN

Buy Bearcoin on the Polygon network using your favorite decentralized exchange. You'll need to keep a balance of at least 100 BTCBEAR in order to be part of the inflation pool.

2) CONNECT TO METAMASK

In the "Your Bearcoin" panel on bearcoin.io, click "Connect to Metamask" and authorize the request in Metamask.

3) SWITCH TO THE CORRECT ACCOUNT IN METAMASK

If you have multiple Metamask accounts, make sure you switch to the account that contains your Bearcoin.

4) ENABLE INFLATION MINING ON YOUR ACCOUNT

In the "Your Bearcoin" panel on bearcoin.io, click "Enable Now" and authorize the transaction using Metamask.

5) SIT BACK AND WAIT FOR INFLATION!

When Bearcoin enters an inflationary cycle, inflation will be minted by looking up 10 random accounts (out of all the accounts that have enabled inflation AND have a balance of 100 BTCBEAR).

The inflation will be mined to whichever of the ten random accounts has the highest balance of Bearcoin.

So more BTCBEAR held in your account means better odds of mining inflation!



THE DOWNSIDE OF ENABLING INFLATION MINING ON AN ACCOUNT

It's important to realize that enabling inflation mining on your account ALSO enables deflation.

When Bearcoin is in a deflationary cycle, transfers to/from this account will be have a small portion of the transaction burned as deflation.

  • For transfers where both accounts have inflation/deflation enabled, the recipient will pay the deflation.
  • For transfers where only the sender has inflation/deflation enabled, the sender will pay the deflation.
  • For transfers where neither account has inflation/deflation enabled, no deflation will occur.

To prevent users from reaping the benefits of inflation mining without having to help with deflation, there's no way to disable inflation mining on an account once it's been enabled.

However, users are free to transfer tokens to a new account that isn't subject to inflation/deflation (though at that point, they will no longer be mining any inflation on the new account).
